Interactive Solar System Graphic

Content Objective:

Create an interactive computer graphic representation of the solar system using HTML and JavaScript. The objective is to provide an engaging and educational experience for users to explore the planets, their orbits, and key information about each celestial body.

Challenges:

  • Rendering Realistic Orbits: Representing the elliptical orbits of planets accurately within the limitations of HTML and JavaScript.
  • Interactive User Experience: Designing intuitive controls for users to navigate and interact with the solar system.
  • Data Integration: Gathering and integrating accurate data about the planets and their characteristics.
  • Performance Optimization: Ensuring smooth performance, especially with complex animations and interactions.
